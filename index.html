Crescent Childcare is a home daycare center, licensed by the State of Illinois, dedicated to providing a high quality, nurturting, and safe learning environment for your child that will help him/her grow into a wonderful, curious, and happy person.

Mission

We provide a secure and safe learning environment for all children that encourages self-expression (care for self) and group responsibility (care for others); offers an area through which your children may discover and express their personal potential through exploration and experimentation. It is important for parents to feel good about bringing their children to a safe and nourishing environment. It is our privilege to assure that every child experiences these basic rights.

Program

Program

Our program varies to fit the needs of you and your child. We provide high-quality full-time childcare for children and infants, newborn through 12 years. Breakfast, lunch, and snacks are prepared in accordance with federal food guidelines and are served to the children “family style”. Activities are scheduled but we often follow child-initiated activities to encourage curiosity and wonderment of life.

Cirriculum

Our weekly activities are well balanced for group and free play, and consist of learning activities that will help your child develop his/her emotional, physical, intellectual, and social skills. In the morning we allow about an hour of free play before going into circle time. Circle time consists of music and movement followed by a story. Next, we move into the kitchen for our preschool lessons, which include science, Spanish, math, writing, and art activities before we transition them back into free play. In the summer our program is geared towards being outside and enjoying the sun. We go for walks, use water tables, have bubbles, ride bikes, and have picnic lunches in the backyard on child-sized picnic tables.
